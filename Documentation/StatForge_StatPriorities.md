# StatForge — Stat Priority Reference

**Source:** Wowhead (Midnight Season 1)
**Update trigger:** New season, major patch, or significant tuning pass.

---

## Notes

- Where two stats are listed as equal they are shown as `Stat A = Stat B`
- Some specs have priority splits by **content type** (Raid vs Dungeon/Mythic+) or **playstyle** (Survivability vs DPS, Defensive vs Offensive)
- `Item Level` as a priority means the stat it represents (Agility, Strength, Intellect + Stamina/Armor) dominates to the point where ilvl is the real decision driver
- Elemental Shaman's Mastery has a **1200 rating breakpoint** — relevant for the Breakpoints optimization path
